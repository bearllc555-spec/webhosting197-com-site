Common questions

What does $147 include?

One website, hosted forever on a globally distributed edge network, with SSL renewing automatically. AI builds the initial site to your spec and keeps the content fresh as your business evolves. Domain registration is separate (~$15/yr at any registrar). No upsells, no monthly fees.

Why is the founder price $147 if you’re called webhosting197?

$197 is the standard lifetime price (right there in the name). Founders — the first 50 signups — lock in $147 instead, $50 off, for life. Once the 50 spots fill, the price reverts to $197.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Bring any domain you already own, or register a fresh one with any registrar. We’ll point it at your hosted site at setup.

Who owns the website?

You do. Site, content, domain — all yours. If you ever want to leave, you take everything with you.

What if I want to edit the site myself?

You can. Most people just tell the AI what to change (“add a winter menu,” “swap the photo of the storefront,” “raise prices to $X”) and it ships the change. Manual editing is also available for anything you’d rather hand-tune.

What if my business changes?

Same answer — tell the AI. New services, new hours, new pricing, new team photos, seasonal content. Your hosting plan doesn’t change. Edits are unlimited.
